WHICH ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES ARE RIGHT FOR YOU?

We offer three different forms of injectable anti-wrinkle treatments, and we can help you to decide which one is right for you. During your consultation, your Doctor will assess your skin and listen to your concerns before recommending the best injectables for you, based on the three below.

BOTOX® Injections
Made from a drug called Botulinum Toxin, BOTOX® injections relax the muscle to stop lines and wrinkles forming.

We target only the small, specific muscles responsible for unwanted lines and wrinkles, meaning your normal facial expressions will remain unaffected. Administered correctly by a medical practitioner they can subtly smooth and soften the skin, restoring a youthful complexion.

Juvéderm®
Juvéderm® is made from biocompatible, biodegradable materials and contains Hyaluronic Acid, which replaces the naturally existing substance in your skin that repairs damaged tissue.

There are different types of Juvéderm®:
  • Juvéderm® For Lips fills in lip lines and wrinkles whilst hyaluronic acid attracts and retains moisture. Results can last up to a year.
  • Juvéderm® VOLUMA® with Lidocaine, replenishes lost skin volume to regain a youthful curve to the cheeks and can be used to enhance the overall shape of your face.
  • Juvéderm® Hydrate™ helps attract and hold moisture within the skin to restore a healthy natural glow.

Sculptra™
Sculptra™ is composed of Poly-L-lactic acid – a substance formed in the body when you exercise and a synthetic material found in dissolvable stitches and various implants.

Sculptra™ is used to improve the appearance of hollowed cheeks and sunken eyes. A progressive increase in volume will become apparent as the dermal thickness expands and gives you a fuller, more youthful and fresh-faced look.

WHAT IS IN AN ANTI-AGEING INJECTION?

At Facial Aesthetix, you can rest assured that we only use BOTOX® injections, which are made of a neurotoxin drug derived from the bacterium, Clostridium Botulinum, and Juvederm®, which is mainly Hyaluronic Acid. Hyaluronic Acid is a substance naturally found in the body.

WILL I BE ABLE TO MOVE MY FACE AFTER HAVING ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES?

Yes. The frozen look is only caused when too many BOTOX® injections are administered into one area. Working under the strictest regulation in the UK, exceeding all mandatory and voluntary requirements, Facial Aesthetix practitioners are expertly qualified to prescribe the correct dosage and frequency of treatment. We target only the small, specific muscles responsible for unwanted lines and wrinkles, meaning your normal facial expressions will remain unaffected. During your consultation your doctor or nurse will discuss the best plan for you to achieve your desired look.

WHAT ARE ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES?

Anti-Ageing Injectables involve injecting a medically developed solution into your facial muscle with a fine needle. There are different types of injectables, including fillers and Botulinum Toxin, commonly known as BOTOX® injections. Anti-Ageing Injectables relax the muscle to stop lines and wrinkles forming as well have lifting and filling areas. When administered correctly by a medical practitioner they can subtly smooth and soften the skin, restoring a youthful complexion.

ARE ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES SAFE?

Absolutely. BOTOX® injections in the face are FDA and MHRA (US Food and Drug Administration, UK Medicines and Products Healthcare Regulatory Agency) approved. BOTOX® injections and fillers have been used for many years with no known adverse effects. Like most cosmetic treatments, side effects rarely occur. Mild bruising and swelling is sometimes reported and a small percentage of users (1-3%) will experience drooping brows or eyelids. Both can be corrected and are temporary. To reduce the risk of complication, it is vital that you have anti-ageing injectables in a safe and medical environment. At Facial Aesthetix, your treatment will be carried out only by a registered healthcare professional.

HOW DO ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES WORK?

When you move your face – laugh, concentrate, squint or frown - your muscles contract. After years of frequent contraction, combined with environmental and lifestyle factors, lines and wrinkles form. Muscles contract due to a signal sent from the brain via nerves. BOTOX® injections work by interrupting this signal at the muscle, leaving them relaxed and unable to crease the skin.
Also, as we age the fat pads in our face drop and move downwards, creating a sagging effect and folds in the skin. When fillers are injected at strategic points around the face, they lift the skin and smooth out folds.

DO ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES HURT?

Anti-ageing injectables are virtually pain free and require no downtime. Unlike traditional anti-ageing procedures, such as surgical face lifts, injectables requires no cuts, incisions or stitches. Many clients describe the sensation as a small scratch from a tiny needle. For those feeling anxious, we can also use a local anaesthetic to numb the area completely.

WHAT HAPPENS AT MY CONSULTATION?

It’s important to us that you achieve the results you want, and we believe this starts with a great consultation. You will never be rushed and our highly trained practitioners will take the time to discuss aspects of your skin you may be unhappy with, your desired results, costs and timings. Our aim is for you to leave your consultation feeling confident, fully informed and looking forward to your treatment.

WHERE CAN I HAVE ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES?

Popular areas to have anti-ageing injectables include around the eyes, forehead and between the eyebrows to target lines and wrinkles. However, increasingly BOTOX® injections and fillers are being used to lift eyebrows and corners of the mouth and to target the orange peel appearance of the chin.

WHAT RESULTS WILL I SEE?

Your facial lines and wrinkles will appear smoothed out, giving you a subtly revived appearance. Your doctor or nurse will discuss your specific concerns and the results you can expect to see in your consultation.

HOW LONG WILL THE RESULTS LAST?

Results can take two weeks to reach their full effect and can last up to four months. However, this varies depending on a number of factors, which will be discussed with you in your consultation.

DO ANTI-AGEING INJECTABLES REALLY PREVENT AGEING?

When used regularly, it has been reported that Anti-Ageing Injectables can delay the signs of ageing. As we know, facial muscle movement causes lines and wrinkles. Anti-ageing injectables can restrict this movement, preventing lines, fold and wrinkles from forming.
